DPO Group is Africa’s biggest PSP

DPO Group is the leading African payment service provider (PSP). We enable businesses and individuals across the continent to accept

payments online with all currencies and payment methods, including all major cards, mobile money, and e-wallets. DPO Group currently

works with upwards of 100,000 merchants, across 19 countries, and has local offices in Cape Town and Johannesburg. Founded in 2006, it

has made a series of strategic acquisitions including, in 2016, PayGate and 2019, PayFast, South Africa’s most prominent PSPs.
